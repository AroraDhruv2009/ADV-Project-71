National Science Day
National Science Day

National Science Day was held in order to commemorate the invention of the Raman Effect in India by the Indian Physicist, Sir Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman in the year 1928. The theme was ‘India: Torchbearer against COVID’ . A short video was shared online by science teachers on MS-teams during the online classes. Students enthusiastically participated in an array of activities like poster making, creating animated video, painting and sculpture.

National Youth Day
National Youth Day

On the occasion of the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekanand, the students of Delhi Public School Ghaziabad participated in various activities. The main objective of the celebration was to propagate the philosophy and the ideals of Swami Vivekananda and to promote rational thinking among the youth of the country.

The following activities were conducted to celebrate this day on 12th January 2021-
1.Students of classes 6 to 8 wrote poems, essays and speeches on the ideals of Swami Vivekanand and, how as youth of the country they can incorporate those ideals in their life. A movie on the life story of Swami Vivekanand was shown to the students. The life story of Swami Vivekanand not only inspired the young brigade but also gave them an insight about their duties towards the country.
2. An online painting competition was also organized for the students of classes 7 to 9.
3.The students of classes 9 and 10 participated enthusiastically in Hindi Essay Writing competition.
4.A webinar was also organised for the students of class 8. Eminent personality Mr.Krishna Adhikary, the Secretary General of Universal Peace Federation addressed the students.

  • DPSG FIGHTS COVID19

    Mindfully Yours Greetings from DPSG!

    These are uncertain times lot of unanswered questions come in the mind of students and parents alike. There is a lot of fear and anxiety about this new disease and what could happen can be overwhelming and it can give rise to lot of strong and unexplained emotions in adults and children. Social isolation during the pandemic is also one of the contributing factors of stress and anxiety. Moderate levels Stress and Anxiety is normal during these times, as this will force us all to take all precautionary measures required like sanitization and washing of hands. However, we have to learn to differentiate between Productive and unproductive Anxiety thinking continuously about the disease will only increase our stress levels and will make us Panic During this time.

    Delhi Public school Ghaziabad wants to help and guide its students in dealing with these difficult times. Here are some tips which will help students in Coping with the current situation.

  • Fit India

    On 14 December 2020, Delhi Public School Ghaziabad Meerut Road started the Fit India School Week Celebration 2020. The aim of FITNESS WEEK is to instill in students the understanding of regular physical activity and higher levels of fitness. In the process, the school aims at enhancing self-esteem and confidence in the students.

    Unlike the previous year, this year ‘Fit India School Week’ is being celebrated virtually. The activities planned for the first day were Virtual Assembly, FreeHand Exercises, and Dance. Teachers and students actively participated and joined the movement called FIT INDIA.
